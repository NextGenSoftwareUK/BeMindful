THE VISION

MINDFULNESS AS A SUPERPOWER

Be Mindful began as a module planned for Our World — a way to reward genuine spiritual practice with real in-game power. It grew into a standalone OAPP that sits at the heart of the OASIS wellness ecosystem, connecting every mindful moment to karma and cross-game progression.

🧠
REAL PRACTICE, REAL REWARDS

Every session you complete earns karma — not simulated karma, but the same karma score that powers your avatar across Our World, One World, ODOOM and OQUAKE. The calmer and more consistent your practice, the stronger you become across the entire OASIS.

📊
YOUR WELLBEING, TRACKED

Log your mood before and after each session and watch it plotted on a live line graph over days, weeks and months. Your mood directly affects your avatar's aura and glow in every OASIS game — higher wellbeing means a more radiant, powerful presence.

Group sessions multiply the effect. The more people practising in an area, the higher that area's collective vibration rises.

MEDITATION

THE ORIGIN

BUILT ONCE BEFORE

DAVID ELLAMS · 2012

Be Mindful was first designed and nearly fully built in 2012 — a Windows 8 Metro app, built during David's contracting days on the commute into work and back home again. The UI was done. The breathing guide worked. The mood tracker was wired up. It was nearly ready to ship.

Then his laptop was stolen. Every line of code gone. At the time he was more upset about losing the code than the laptop — the laptop was easy to replace. The code wasn't. After that he started backing everything up every night, auto-syncing to his PC and OneDrive. He never lost code like that again.

He burned out not long after and was ill for two years from 2013. Be Mindful never got rebuilt. But it was never forgotten — it was always on the list, always planned, always meant to exist.

Thirteen years later, it's here. Not as a Windows 8 Metro app but as a full OASIS OAPP — connected to the karma engine, to Our World, to every other app in the Omniverse. Worth the wait.

DAVID ELLAMS — CREATOR

"I always believed the best version of yourself in real life should be the best version of yourself in the game. Be Mindful is how that happens. Sit in stillness. Breathe deeply. Move with intention. And watch your power grow — not just in the game, but in the world."
